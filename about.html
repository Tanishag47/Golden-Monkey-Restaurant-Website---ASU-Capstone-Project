Founded in 2022, as a family. Over the last 4 years (& counting), we've established a thriving business that we are proud to call our second home.

Our Roots

At the heart of Golden Monkey Cafe is a story of family, resilience, and cultural pride. Founded by Chenda Toeung and her husband Peter, the café is deeply rooted in Cambodian heritage and personal journey. After moving to the United States from Phnom Penh in 2011, Chenda brought with her generations of culinary tradition. Her Mother later joined her side in the kitchen. All together, they created a space where recipes, language, and memories are preserved and shared. Their story is not just about opening a restaurant—it’s about honoring where they come from while building something meaningful in Lynn, MA.

Our Cuisine

Our menu is a celebration of authentic Cambodian flavors—bold, balanced, and deeply rooted in tradition. While often compared to neighboring cuisines, Cambodian food has its own identity, built on fresh herbs, rich broths, and signature ingredients like lemongrass. Cambodian cuisine blends sweet, savory, and aromatic elements into dishes that feel both comforting and vibrant. From beloved street foods like noodle dishes to family recipes like mekathang, every plate is crafted with intention and care. At Golden Monkey Cafe, we aim not only to serve delicious food, but to introduce and educate our guests on the beauty and uniqueness of Cambodian cooking.

Our Community

Golden Monkey Cafe is more than a place to eat—it's a place to connect. Located in the diverse city of Lynn, the café embraces the opportunity to share Cambodian culture with a wider community. Whether celebrating traditions like the Cambodian New Year or simply welcoming guests through the door, the goal is always the same: to bring people together through food and hospitality. Built on family values and cultural pride, Golden Monkey Cafe continues to serve as a gathering place where stories are shared, traditions are honored, and everyone is invited to feel at home.
